What's the Goal?

As a person that loves crafting and creating art during my free time, I often find myself struggling with art block. I noticed that there are also other artists that experience the same problem as I do. Using this website, I hope to assist other artists and provide them with some ideas or creative prompts for their next artwork. I also included an inspiration board for artists that need ideas for specific themes or aesthetics, and as well as a page for "craft challenges" for artists that want to test their crafty, artistic minds and create artworks within the specific conditions of each challenge.
