Who Am I?

My name is Mikaela Denise T. Martin, and I am a 14 year old (as of 2025) art enthusiast. I love seeing other people's creative flow and understanding how they express themselves through their masterpieces. Their creativity inspires me to make my own artworks and let my hands create depictions of my thoughts and feelings.


I don't create art to compete. I create art because it brings me to my safe and happy place. I realize that it's not only a hobby, but it's also something that helps calm me down and cures my boredom. For me, art helps me escape from reality and enter the realm of my own mind. It allows me to understand myself better, as well as it gives me the freedom to think and be alone with my thoughts in a comforting way.


Every stroke I make with a paintbrush, and every piece of plastic I glue together, helps me realize and discover more about myself. I hope that in time, both my skills and passion in art will grow as I do.
